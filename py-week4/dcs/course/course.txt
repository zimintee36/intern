Course Introduction
The Diploma in Computer Science programme offers a 2-year study encompassing the Computer Science discipline, providing students with a broad spectrum of computer knowledge. The curriculum covers areas such as Programming, Network, Operating System, Computer Architecture, and Mathematics for computer science.
Throughout the programme, students acquire a solid foundation in computing theory and practical skills, along with a broader understanding of Computer Science, preparing them for both current market demands and future professional development.
Upon successful completion of this Diploma programme with a CGPA of 2.5 or above and meeting the credit transfer requirements, you will be eligible to admit into Year 2 of the following degree programmes offered at New Era University College:
Bachelor of Computer Science (Hons) in Software Engineering
Bachelor of Computer Science (Hons) in Software Engineering , with the selection of elective tracks in: Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Applied Generative AI Internet-of-Things (IoT) Big Data and Data Analytics Extended Reality (XR)

Minimum Entry Requirement
Possess SPM with at least THREE (3) credits in any subject (inclusive of Mathematics or any equivalent qualification).
Possess UEC with at least THREE (3) credits in any subject (inclusive of Mathematics or any       equivalent qualification).
* A Certificate (Level 3, MQF) in a related field with at least a CGPA of 2.00.
O Level / IGCSE / Other qualifications deemed equivalent by MQA.
* Candidates with a pass in Mathematics at an SPM level or its equivalent may be admitted if the admission qualification contains subjects in Mathematics that are equivalent to Mathematics at SPM level.
*For detailed information on entry requirements, please contact Marketing & Sales Department .
