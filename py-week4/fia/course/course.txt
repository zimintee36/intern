Course Introduction
The Foundation in Arts (FIA) Programme at New Era University College (NEUC) is a full-time, one-year programme designed to equip students with the essential skills needed for success across various fields. This programme emphasises the development of critical competencies, including strategic thinking, effective communication, and resource management. Additionally, students will gain practical experience in analysing complex issues, solving real-world problems, and collaborating across disciplines.
The programme offers exposure to diverse fields such as Business and Accountancy, Mass Communication, Design, and Education. In the competitive world of the arts, professionals and executives must demonstrate a broad range of skills, knowledge, and adaptability to thrive in an ever-changing environment. Upon completing this programme, students will be well-prepared to pursue a variety of Bachelor’s degrees at NEUC.

Career Prospects
Education Pathway
The Foundation in Arts (FIA) Programme at New Era University College (NEUC) is meticulously designed to provide students with the foundational knowledge and essential skills needed for a seamless transition into undergraduate studies at NEUC. Serving as a robust stepping stone, this programme offers students a comprehensive introduction to their chosen fields of study. More than just a pathway to a degree, the FIA Programme marks the beginning of a transformative educational journey that prepares students for academic success and future professional achievements.
Upon completing this programme, students may advance to pursue a variety of Bachelor’s degrees at our university college, including:
Bachelor of Arts (Honours) in Chinese Language & Literature
Bachelor in Early Childhood Education (Honours)
Bachelor of Education (Teaching Chinese as a Second Language) (Honours)
Bachelor of Arts (Honours) in Business Administration
Bachelor of Business in Marketing (Honours)
Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Finance and Accounting
Bachelor of Arts (Honours) in Visual Communication
Bachelor of Arts (Honours) in Media and Communication Studies
